About Full stack Restaurant

As full-stack restaurant, we bring the rich flavors of North Indian and South Indian cuisines under one roof. From farm-fresh ingredients to dine-in, takeaway, and online delivery, we manage every step to serve authentic taste with seamless service.

Spread across 25 countries with 150+ outlets, Full stack Restaurant is proudly one of the world's largest restaurants with headquarters at Chennai (Tamil Nadu, India).
